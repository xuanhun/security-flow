## Case Card Contract

Each case card should include:

- challenge metadata;
- original prompt summary;
- category and pattern;
- signal inventory;
- probes and evidence;
- route decision;
- flag or final answer when it is safe to store;
- links back to the reusable knowledge pattern and local evidence.
